What is MCCOY ?

Mccoy is a professionally managed company engaged in marketing of pharmaceutical, Ayurvedic and health care based products. Mccoy started its operation in the year 1998 to provide top quality effective products. Mccoy is committed to serve the mankind with quality products. Mccoy product portfolio includes prescription medicine and ayurvedic products. Its prescription medicine range across therapeutic areas such as gynaecology , diabetic , General diseases like anti-inflammatory ,anti spasmodic , pain, constipation , abdominal disorders , cough and cold and others.
